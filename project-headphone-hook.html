About this project

A simple wall-mounted headphone hook designed in TinkerCAD and printed in bright green PLA. Mounts flat against the wall with a curved arm that holds the headphone band securely without scratching it.

A good example of using 3D printing to solve an everyday problem instead of buying a solution.
