About this Project

A fully custom protective case for AirPods 4, designed from scratch in Fusion 360 to fit the exact dimensions of the AirPods 4 charging case.

Features a two piece hinged shell in white PLA with green trim accents. The case includes diagonal texture for grip, corner guards for drop protection, and a functional keychain loop.

Designed with proper fit testing to ensure the AirPods 4 fit snugly without forcing, while maintaining ease of opening and closing.
