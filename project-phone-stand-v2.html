About this Project

The second and improved version of the phone stand, redesigned from scratch in Fusion 360. Unlike V1, this stand features an adjustable angle mechanism with a ratcheted base that locks the phone panel at multiple viewing angles. The wider design also accommodates larger phones comfortably.


Took significantly more time to design (5 hours) due to the adjustable hinge mechanism, but the result is a much more versatile and polished stand. A clear example of iteration and improvement between versions.
